  • Who can participate?

    Everyone is welcome to apply, be it students, professionals or turing-test certified androids. If you are under 18 years of age, well need a parental consent form.

  • How does the application process work?

    Were looking for people who "do"! Folks who are passionate enough to work on crazy world-changing ideas. Tell us what makes you apply to Wittyhacks, what excites you, accomplishments you are proud of and whatever else you think can strengthen your chances of acceptance. Well get to know more about your abilities from the past projects, GitHub profile, participation/awards in other hackathons.

  • Can we apply as a team?

    Yes! This hackathon is strictly a team competition. We believe that youre stronger as a team than you are apart. Teams must have at least 3 members and at most 4 members.

  • What if I dont have a team or an idea?

    Not to worry, most people dont! Well have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with. You can join our Facebook Group to find potential teammates before you register.

  • What shouldnt I bring?

    Weapons of any kind, drugs, or alcohol. If youre not sure whether something will be okay, please ask ahead of time!

  • What about hardware?

    If you have specific hardware in mind, bring it with you. We will be doing our best to lend hardware at the event and enable you to work on your dream hack. Well put out a list closer to the event so you can see whats available.

  • I dont want to hack, can I still get involved?

    You can help us by spreading the word about WittyHacks to your friends and acquaintances. If you are someone with specialized technical skills, then reach out to us via email to become a mentor. If your company is interested in becoming a sponsor, Fill up the Sponsorship form.

  • When will applications close?

    The application portal will be closed on December 31st at 11:59pm IST. We evaluate applications on a rolling basis and acceptances will be sent every weekend via email. The sooner you apply, the better chances you have of acceptance. Once accepted, youll have five days to confirm your attendance.

  • How much does it cost?

    Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Admission to Wittyhacks is completely free, thanks to our sponsors! We'll provide you with a weekend's worth of schwag, meals, drinks, snacks and a place to crash when you need a break from coding.

  • How big can a team be?

    You can form teams of up to 4 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills.

  • Once I am accepted, what do I need to bring?

    A valid government issued photo ID, laptop, phone, chargers, any sleeping equipment you might want (we'll be providing mattresses) and a geeky t-shirt!

  • Do you provide travel reimbursements?

    No! We can't provide travel reimbursements to praticipants. Incase you arrive early on 18th, we can host you at wittyfeed office.

  • What do I get by attending Wittyhacks?

    Five word: Experience you will never forget.
